About me

I am a Data Scientist and analyst with a passion for transforming complex data into actionable insights. Currently pursuing a Master of Science in Data Science with a concentration in Computational Methods at DePaul University, I combine theoretical knowledge with practical applications to tackle challenging problems. I specialize in machine learning, deep learning, and data analysis and visualization techniques.

My experience spans both technical implementation and strategic operations, having successfully managed a retail operation with over 10,000 SKUs and developed an e-commerce platform that significantly increased sales. I specialize in machine learning model development, predictive analytics, and ETL pipeline engineering using technologies like Python, AWS, and Apache Spark.

My recent projects include developing high-performance prediction models for medical applications and implementing end-to-end data solutions that drive business value. I'm continuously exploring the intersection of data science and practical applications, with particular interest in healthcare analytics and business intelligence.
